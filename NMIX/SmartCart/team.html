Our Story

Smartcart originated as an idea developed in New Media class at UGA. After the conclusion of the class, the team continued development of Smartcart in a dorm room. It has been two years since then and now Smartcart is a multimillion dollar company! Below is our wonderful team that has made Smartcart possible.

Cece Bailey

Cece Bailey is the Founder and CEO of Smartcart, which she started from her dorm room in 2013. During the company’s early years, Cece acted as the sole engineer, designer, and support representative for the entire Smartcart platform, allowing for it to be a stable business from the outset. Cece holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Georgia.

Bowen Yang

Bowen brings 20 years of technology industry experience to Smartcart’s Finance Team, and has spent much of his career building new or transforming existing businesses. He holds an MBA from the University of Georgia.

Becca Wright

As the leader of Smartcart's Strategy division, Becca is responsible for developing the analytical frameworks that shape the company’s business. Since joining Smartcart, she has grown the division into a team that covers everything from business strategy and customer insights to analytics and data science. Becca graduated from University of Georgia, where he majored in Psychology and was an editor at the Red and Black Daily News.

Mary Sancomb

As the Chief Creative Officer of Smartcart, Mary leads the company’s award-winning creative team, whose work encapsulates all brand creative, brand design and product design efforts for the company. Earlier in her career, she worked as a Creative Director at a smaller company. She graduated from University of Georgia with a BFA in graphic design.
